# App Screenshots

Add your app screenshots here with these exact filenames:

1. **discover.png** - The Discover tab showing sets
2. **feed.png** - The Feed tab with activity
3. **identify.png** - The track identification modal/screen
4. **crate.png** - The Crate tab (saved sets, liked tracks)
5. **profile.png** - The Profile tab

## Recommended Screenshot Specs

- **Resolution**: 1170 x 2532 (iPhone 14 Pro) or similar
- **Format**: PNG (transparent backgrounds OK)
- **Aspect Ratio**: ~9:19.5 (standard iPhone)

## How to Capture Screenshots

### Option 1: iOS Simulator
1. Run `npx expo start`
2. Press `i` to open iOS Simulator
3. Navigate to each screen
4. Press `Cmd + S` to save screenshot

### Option 2: Physical Device
1. Navigate to each screen
2. Press Side Button + Volume Up simultaneously
3. AirDrop or email screenshots to your computer

### Option 3: Expo Go
1. Take screenshots on your device
2. Export from Photos app

## Tips for Best Results

- Use dark mode (the commercial is designed for dark backgrounds)
- Show populated states (not empty screens)
- Capture during "wow" moments (track being identified, etc.)
- Make sure notifications are cleared
- Hide status bar time if possible (or make it clean like 9:41)
