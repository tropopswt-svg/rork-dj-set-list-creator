How to Bulk Scrape Sets

1
Click a DJ's "Sets" button to open their 1001Tracklists page
2
Cmd+Click (Mac) or Ctrl+Click (Win) on individual set links to open them in new tabs
3
Wait for the notification on each tab (3-5 seconds), then close it
4
Repeat! Each set you scrape adds to your database with full tracklist + timestamps
